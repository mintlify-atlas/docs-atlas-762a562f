---

OpenRefine is a powerful desktop application that runs in your browser, giving you full control over messy data without sending it to any external server. Whether you're cleaning a spreadsheet, transforming JSON, or reconciling values against Wikidata, OpenRefine handles it all with a visual, interactive workflow that keeps a complete history of every change you make.
